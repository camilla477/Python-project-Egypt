🔍 Key Findings

  • Shift from Peaceful to Confrontational: Peaceful protests decreased from 51.2% (2011) to 35.3% (2019-2020), while riots increased from 24.9% to 37.4%.
  • Geographic Concentration: Cairo dominated both periods with 1,457 events (2011) and 1,124 events (2019-2020).
  • Strategic Activities Rising: Strategic developments increased from 5.9% (2011) to 14% (2019-2020).
  • Reduced Overall Activity: Total events declined by 42% from 2011 to 2019-2020, suggesting fewer but more intense protests.
